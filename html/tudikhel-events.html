About the Festival

The Tudikhel Food Carnival is a vibrant celebration of food, culture, and community held at the heart of Kathmandu. Bringing together popular street food vendors, traditional Nepali cuisine, and international flavors, the festival offers a lively atmosphere with live performances, games, and activities for all age groups. Designed as a family-friendly event, the carnival aims to create a fun and memorable experience while supporting local food entrepreneurs and artists.
