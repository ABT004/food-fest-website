Tokha Food Fest

Tokha, Kathmandu \ Near Bhootkhel Ground

8th & 9th January, 2026

1 PM to 9 PM

About the Festival

Tokha Food Fest is a community-driven celebration of food, culture, and local businesses. This year's festival brings together traditional Newari cuisine, popular street foods, live cultural performances, and family-friendly activities in the heart of Tokha. From mouth-watering local delicacies to exciting games and live entertainment, the festival promises an unforgettable experience for all age groups.
