Bouddha Food Fest

Bouddha, Kathmandu \ Near Bouddha Stupa

15th & 16th January, 2026

1 PM to 9 PM

About the Festival

The Boudha Street Food & Culture Fest is a relaxed, evening-focused food festival held around the iconic Boudhanath area. Blending local street food with international flavors, the festival creates a welcoming space for families, tourists, and food lovers to enjoy good food, soft music, and cultural experiences. With its peaceful atmosphere and vibrant surroundings, the event offers a unique food festival experience in one of Kathmandu's most popular cultural hubs.

  • Kids' activities and simple fun games
