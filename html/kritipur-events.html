About the Festival

The Kritipur Food & Culture Fest is a cultural-focused celebration that highlights the rich Newari heritage of Kritipur through food, music, and traditional performances. Set against the historic surroundings of Kritipur, the festival brings together authentic local cuisine, traditional art, and community-led entertainment. The event is designed to preserve local culture while offering visitors a warm, festive experience filled with flavor and tradition.
